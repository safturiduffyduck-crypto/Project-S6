Our Heritage

LYS Girls Series

Inspired by the emotional universe of Diary of Dreary, this design tells a quiet story about love that fades but never fully disappears. The illustration captures a moment of inner conflict when someone wants to hold on, yet understands that forcing someone to stay would only break what once felt safe. The character visuals reflect vulnerability, overthinking, and the silent weight of memories that linger long after goodbye becomes inevitable.

The phrase “I can’t say goodbye to you, but I can’t let myself force you” represents the core theme often found in Diary of Dreary’s music: accepting loss with sincerity, even when the heart resists. Meanwhile, the visual storytelling of the girl in solitude symbolizes emotional isolation, a recurring narrative in the band’s identity where sadness becomes art, and honesty becomes comfort. Both the t-shirt and sticker are designed not just as merchandise, but as fragments of a diary a reminder that feeling lost is part of being human, and sometimes the deepest emotions are the ones never fully spoken.

Filan Akbar Lazuardi

Creative Director
